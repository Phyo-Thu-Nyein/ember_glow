Our Story

Ember Glow Hotel began its journey in 1990 as a humble two-story building with just four rooms, started from scratch by a passionate family with a dream of providing unparalleled hospitality. Nestled in the heart of the city, our hotel has grown and evolved significantly over the years, offering a unique blend of charm, luxury, and modern amenities. Our dedication to excellence and personalized service has made us a beloved destination for travelers from around the world.

Timeline of Milestones

  • 1990

     Ember Glow Hotel opens its doors as a quaint 4-room inn.

  • 1995

     Expansion adds 30 new rooms and a fine dining restaurant, "The Ember Dine."

  • 2000

    The hotel's first major renovation introduces modern amenities and luxurious suites.

  • 2005

    Launch of the state-of-the-art fitness center and wellness spa.

  • 2010

     Introduction of eco-friendly initiatives, including solar panels and waste reduction programs.

  • 2015

     Opened the conference and event center, catering to business travelers and event planners.

  • 2020

    Celebrated 30 years of excellence with a newly refurbished lobby and lounge area.
